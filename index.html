Our Fleet

Boeing 757-200

Our first modern airliner. She can carry up to 150 Economy passangers, 20 Business class passangers and up to 8,700kg of cargo.

Douglas DC3

We have two of these classical beauties. They can each carry up to 28 passangers who are all treated like a Business Class passangers with only Economy Class price. Also carries up to 1,400kg of cargo.
